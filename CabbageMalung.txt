CABBAGE MALUNG
30 grams mustard seeds
60 grams desiccated coconut
1 medium onion finely chopped
1 Savoy cabbage finely chopped
Method:
Mix together cabbage coconut and onion in a large mixing bowl.
In a dry ovenproof tray, heat the mustard seeds to release flavour. This usually takes a couple of minutes
Add the mixed veg place to the tray and place in the middle of a medium oven uncovered. Dry roast for 45 mins, stirring every 15 mins or so.
