The Glombek's Cider Recipe

3lb apples1lb
12 pts water4 pts
2 lemons2/3
2lb sugar2/3

Chop up the apples in a mincer or food processor

Put in a bucket with the water

Stir morning and night for one week

Strain and add the lemons and sugar

Leave 24 hours

Strain

Bottle in plastic fizzy drink bottles

Ready to drink in one week
