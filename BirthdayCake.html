Birthday Cake

Gas Mark 3, 160C, 325F
  2lb fruit tin
1 litre pudding basin		 8in sandwich tins 8in square tin
Soft margarine 4 oz 8 oz 10 oz
Caster sugar 4 oz 8 oz 10 oz
Eggs, size 3 2 4 5
Baking powder none 1 tsp 1 tsp
Self raising flour 6 oz 10 oz 12 oz
Evapourated milk 3 tbsp 4 tbsp 4 tbsp
Vanilla essence 1/2 tsp 1 tsp 1 tsp
Cooking time 45-55 min 1-1/4 hours 1-1/2 hours

Method

Pre-heat the oven.

Grease and line the cake tin.

Put everything into a bowl/food processor and beat/process for 1-2 minutes until smooth.

Bake in the centre of the oven.

Freezes well.
