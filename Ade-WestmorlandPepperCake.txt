
Westmorland Pepper Cake

ingredients

75 g (3 oz) butter
75 g (3 oz) raisins
75 g (3 oz) currants
100 g (4 oz) caster sugar
150ml water
225 g (8 oz) self-raising flour
2.5 ml (1/2 tsp) ground ginger
pinch of ground cloves
2.5 ml (1/2 tsp) finely ground black pepper
1 egg, beaten
60 ml (4 tbsp) fresh milk


method
1. Grease the base of a deep 18 cm (7 inch) round cake tin and line the base with greaseproof paper.

2. Put the butter in a pan, add the fruit, sugar and water and bring to the boil.

3. Put flour, spices and pepper in a bowl and add the egg amd milk before gently stirring in the fruit mixture

3. Turn the mixture into the prepared tin and bake at 180°C (350°F) mark 4 for about 35 minutes or until firm to the touch and golden brown. Turn out and leave to cool on a wire rack.
