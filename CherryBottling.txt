
Syrup strength - 1kg sugar to 3 pints water

preheat oven (300F, 150C, Gas 2)

Pack the cherries into the jars and fill with boiling syrup. Put on the lids and rubber rings. Place the jars in the oven and cook for 50 mins. Remove the jars from the oven and fit immediately with screw bands or clips. Leave for 24 hours and test.
